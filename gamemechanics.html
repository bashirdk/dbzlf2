In Little Fighter 2, A character has defense at 1.0 and health set to 500. A punch usually does 20 damage.
Goku (Buu) has an attack modifier of 1.15x and defense modifier of 2.30x
That means his punch would do 23 damage instead of 20 and a punch that does 20 damage would only inflict 9 damage to him.
